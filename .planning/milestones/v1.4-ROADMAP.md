# Milestone v1.4: Video & Audio Wedge

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-05-05
**Phases:** 23-28
**Total Plans:** 27

## Overview

v1.4 extended Rindle from image-first to image+video+audio without widening the
core lifecycle model. The milestone shipped a system-FFmpeg-backed AV processor,
FFprobe-driven analysis, typed AV domain fields, AV HTML helpers, LiveView
progress/cancellation contracts, and a public onboarding/CI proof lane that
exercises real smartphone-source media through the canonical adopter flow.

## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- Use system FFmpeg subprocesses (FFmpex + MuonTrap) instead of Membrane, NIFs,
  or bundled providers.
- Keep a single `media_assets` table with `:kind` and first-class
  `media_variants` rows with `:output_kind`.
- Reserve `Rindle.Delivery.streaming_url/3` now so future provider adapters can
  land without template churn.
- Keep HLS/DASH/DRM/live streaming/provider adapters out of core v1.4 scope.

**Issues Resolved:**

- Closed the image-only backward-compat risk while extending the profile DSL.
- Added deterministic worker/output verification to guard stale-source and
  silent-truncation failure modes.
- Locked the public AV onboarding story into docs, CI, and the canonical
  adopter lifecycle lane.

**Issues Deferred:**

- Full external-object-store verification matrix beyond the focused public
  surface lane.
- Facade-only cleanup of the first-run adopter upload path.
- Provider-delegated streaming adapters, ABR/HLS/DASH, and GCS/tus carry-over
  work.

**Technical Debt Incurred:**

- `.planning/STATE.md` lagged the executed milestone state and was corrected at
  archive time.

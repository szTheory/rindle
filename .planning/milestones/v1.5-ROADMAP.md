# Milestone v1.5: Adopter Hardening & Lifecycle Repair

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-05-06
**Phases:** 29-32
**Total Plans:** 14

## Overview

v1.5 turned the initial AV wedge into a more boring, operator-grade adoption
story before widening Rindle's support matrix again. The milestone proved the
published package-consumer image and AV happy paths, added explicit lifecycle
repair surfaces, exposed runtime diagnostics for drift and stuck work, and
locked the pre-v1.4 upgrade path into docs and CI-backed proof.

## Phases

## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- Keep the generated-app package-consumer harness as the single truth source
  for built-artifact, published-Hex, AV, and upgrade proofs.
- Make repair operations explicit and asset-scoped on the public facade rather
  than hiding recovery behind broad maintenance or undocumented internals.
- Split runtime concerns cleanly: `mix rindle.doctor` validates setup and
  drift, `mix rindle.runtime_status` reports degraded work, and repair verbs
  perform change.
- Delay breadth work such as GCS, provider adapters, and tus until the current
  AV lifecycle is boring to adopt and operate.

**Issues Resolved:**

- Closed the gap between repo-local proof and real package-consumer adoption by
  proving both image-only and AV-enabled installs from shipped artifacts.
- Restored a truthful cancelled-work recovery story across repair APIs, runtime
  diagnostics, upgrade proof, and docs.
- Made capability drift, queue/delivery misconfiguration, migration drift, and
  stuck lifecycle work visible through supported operator surfaces.
- Locked the pre-v1.4 upgrade path into executable docs and CI-backed proof
  instead of leaving it as a narrative-only claim.

**Issues Deferred:**

- Truthful GCS/resumable-upload support.
- Provider-delegated streaming and processing adapters.
- tus or broader resumable protocol expansion unless adopter demand proves it
  is worth widening the boundary.

**Technical Debt Incurred:**

- Phase 29-32 verification and validation artifacts were backfilled during the
  milestone audit rather than emitted earlier in execution.

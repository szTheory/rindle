## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| generated app -> AV runtime path | FFmpeg-dependent behavior must be proved from the installed artifact, not inferred from repo-local AV tests. |
| canonical adopter AV truth -> generated-app AV smoke | The package-consumer AV path must copy the public contract instead of drifting into a smoke-only recipe. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-29-04 | Repudiation | AV package-consumer proof | mitigate | Keep AV proof in generated-app ExUnit with explicit ready-variant and signed-delivery assertions. |
| T-29-05 | Tampering | generated-app AV profile | mitigate | Reuse `Rindle.Profile.Presets.Web` or its explicit equivalent and keep `VideoProfile` as the source pattern. |
| T-29-06 | Denial of Service | over-broad AV smoke scope | mitigate | Keep the generated-app AV proof narrow to the public happy path: upload, process, ready variants, playback-ready output, signed delivery. |
</threat_model>

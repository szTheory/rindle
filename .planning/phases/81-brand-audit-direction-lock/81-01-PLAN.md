# 81-01: Ten-lens audit of prompts/rindle-brand-book.md

## Objective

Pressure-test the AI-deep-research brand book seed with KEEP/TIGHTEN/REWORK/ADD/REMOVE
verdicts across ten lenses (distinctiveness, dev credibility, Elixir ecosystem fit,
graphic design quality, UI/UX buildout, accessibility, voice/microcopy, marketing,
artifact readiness, naming/legal posture). Every major seed section receives a verdict
with rationale. Accessibility claims are verified with measured WCAG ratios, not
assertions.

## Tasks

1. Verify the adoption-demo logo provenance (read the SVG, identify the artwork).
2. Compute WCAG 2.1 contrast ratios for every "good combination" the seed claims, every
   semantic-on-surface pair, and the focus/border non-text pairs.
3. Write `.planning/research/b1.0-brand-audit.md`: executive judgment, brand DNA
   extraction, scorecard, section-by-section verdict table, stress tests, gaps by
   severity, measured contrast table.

## Verification

- Every seed section (§1–21) appears in the verdict table.
- Contrast table contains measured ratios (two decimals) with pass/fail at 4.5/3.0.
- Audit names at least the focus-ring and border non-text failures if measurements
  confirm them.
